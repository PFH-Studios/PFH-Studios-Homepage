Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29.01.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PFH Studios (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit or interact with this website.

1. General Information

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This website is intended to provide information about our services and to allow interested creators to get in touch with us.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

2. Data we collect

We may collect limited personal data when you voluntarily provide it, including but not limited to:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Social media handles
  • Any information you choose to share when contacting us

This data is only collected when you actively reach out to us (for example via contact forms, booking tools, or direct messages).

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries
  • Communicating about potential collaborations
  • Discussing monetization audits, digital product creation, and launch strategies

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Third-Party Services

This website may link to or integrate third-party services such as:

  • Scheduling tools (e.g. Calendly)
  • Social media platforms (e.g. Instagram)
  • Payment or product platforms (e.g. Whop)

Any data processed by these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not control how third parties handle your data.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website itself does not intentionally use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. If hosting or website platforms collect technical or anonymized data, this is handled according to their own privacy standards.

6. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary to fulfill communication or collaboration purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the email listed in the imprint.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email or Instagram.
